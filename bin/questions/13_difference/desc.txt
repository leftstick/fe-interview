* 在当前路径的`difference`下，找到`index.js`，并补全实现代码

## 提示：
### 你的`difference`模块应该完成如下功能：

```javascript
var difference = require('difference');

var arr1 = [3, 6, 9];
var arr2 = [1, 6, 8];

var diff = difference(arr1, arr2);

console.log(diff); // [3, 9]
```
